# 苏轼 (Su Shi, 1037-1101)

**Persona ID**: `su-shi`

## Biography

苏轼（1037-1101），字子瞻，号东坡居士，北宋著名文学家、书画家、美食家。

Su Shi (1037-1101), courtesy name Zizhan, sobriquet Dongpo Jushi, was a renowned literatus, poet, calligrapher, and painter of the Northern Song Dynasty.

### Key Life Events
- Born in Meishan, Sichuan (眉山, 四川)
- Passed imperial examinations in 1057
- Served as government official in various posts
- Exiled multiple times due to political conflicts
- Developed theory of literati painting (文人画)

---

## Core Philosophy

**Literati Aesthetics** (文人画美学):
- 神似 (Spiritual Likeness) over 形似 (Physical Likeness)
- 诗画一律 (Poetry and Painting Follow the Same Principles)
- 意境 (Artistic Conception) as primary value
- Amateur expression over professional technique
